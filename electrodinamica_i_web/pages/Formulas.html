ANÁLISIS VECTORIAL

Operadores Diferenciales

$$\nabla V = \frac{\partial V}{\partial x} \mathbf{a}_x + \frac{\partial V}{\partial y} \mathbf{a}_y + \frac{\partial V}{\partial z} \mathbf{a}_z$$

$$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{\partial A_x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial A_y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}$$

$$\nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \text{det}\begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{a}_x & \mathbf{a}_y & \mathbf{a}_z \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\ A_x & A_y & A_z \end{vmatrix}$$

Ecuaciones Fundamentales

Integral: $$\oint_S \mathbf{D} \cdot d\mathbf{s} = Q$$

Diferencial: $$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{D} = \rho_v$$

Ley de Faraday: $$\nabla \times \mathbf{E} = -\frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t}$$

Ley de Ampere-Maxwell: $$\nabla \times \mathbf{H} = \mathbf{J} + \frac{\partial \mathbf{D}}{\partial t}$$

ELECTROSTÁTICA

Potencial y Campo

LEY DE COULOMB: $$\mathbf{F} = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{q_1 q_2}{r^2} \mathbf{a}_r$$

POTENCIAL: $$V = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \int \frac{\rho dv}{R}$$

ENERGÍA: $$W_E = \frac{1}{2} \int \mathbf{G} \cdot \mathbf{D} dv$$

MAGNETOSTÁTICA

Corriente y Magnetismo

BIOT-SAVART: $$d\mathbf{B} = \frac{\mu_0 I}{4\pi} \frac{d\mathbf{l} \times \mathbf{a}_r}{r^2}$$

TORQUE: $$\mathbf{T} = \mathbf{m} \times \mathbf{B}$$

INDUCCIÓN: $$V_{emf} = -N \frac{d\Phi}{dt}$$
